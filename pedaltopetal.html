Pedal to Petal (P2P) is a plant and pot shop designed to connect with the vibrant spirit of the MZ generation. The branding features lively, charming graphics that create an inviting and joyful atmosphere for young adults starting their plant journey or visiting the shop. With bold shapes, soft colors, and friendly characters, P2P transforms the traditional plant shop experience into something fresh, fun, and accessible to everyone.
